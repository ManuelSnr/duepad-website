Features

Everything the
unpaid admin needed.

01
Automatic reminders
Everyone in the group gets notified 7 days and 2 days before renewal. No one can say they didn't know — and you never have to ask.
02
"Still in?" poll
Each reminder includes a one-tap opt-in. Everyone declares their intent. You stop asking the same question every month.
03
Auto split calculator
When someone opts out, the cost per person updates instantly. No manual maths, no awkward back-and-forth messages.
04
Secure access details
Store credentials once. Visible only to confirmed members. When someone opts out, the group is nudged to update passwords.
05
Shared dashboard
Everyone sees the same view — upcoming renewals, who's confirmed, and what they owe. No one is out of the loop.
